Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 Stick unsalted butter
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1.5 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2.25 cup diced strawberries
